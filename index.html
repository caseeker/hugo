What is Wim Hof Method

Breathing

Known as prana, qi, chi, or energy, it is the life force itself that not many of us understand the depth. With the specific breathing technique, you are guaranteed to feel the vibrant and powerful sensation. Once you learn the technique, it's always available for the rest of your life, for free!

Cold Exposure

Cold is often seen as a harsh and unpleasant, but there are myriads of benefits from cold exposure that are scientifically proven. Fat loss, reduced inflammation, fortified immune system, hormonal balance, and better cardio vascular system to just name a few. As Wim says, "Cold is our warm friend"

Mindset

The third pillar of Wim Hof Method is mindset and commitment. We often hear phrases like "Mind over Matter", and how powerful our mind is, but do not have a clear way to practice. With proper mindset and clear practice with breathing and cold exposure, your mind and body will get to the next level in no time.

It's easier with a community

Wellness journey can be challenging and overwhelming without a community support. There are so much conflicted information out there which can be hard to navigate around. While you are trying out new things to build better habits, if you don't have people around you who don't understand what you are trying to achive it is easy to lose traction and fall back to old habits.

Come join us! Whether you are fighting specific symptoms or simply becoming more conscious of your health, you will find like minded people who are challenging their own limitations in various ways.

Meet your Instructor

Sungho Spark

Instructor

Sungho is a Korean American, a full-time software engineer, and a certified Wim Hof Method instructor.
Working with computers days and nights, he was often getting sick. While he knew deep down that he had to change his lifestyle, he couldn't break his habits of bad diets, computer games, and drinking.
While searching for motivations he was introduced to the Wim Hof Method. The Vice documentary intrigued him so much he had to fly over to Poland to learn from Wim.
Now two years later, he is practicing surfing, Brazilian Jiu Jitsu and yoga on a daily basis.
He claims that the Wim Hof Method has been the key component to shift his lifestyle, and he is excited to share his practice.
