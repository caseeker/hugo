About Sungho

Computer Engineer

Professionlly worked as a computer engineer for 8+ years and previously addicted to computer games and drinking, Sungho understands how unhealthy modern lifestyle can be.

Wim Hof Method

With the power of breathing, cold exposure, and mindset of WHM, Sungho successfully shifted his lifestyle significantly. Now a certified instructor, he is ready to share his practice.

Healthy Addictions

Addictions can be healthy! Sungho replaced his gaming addiction with Surfing, Jiu Jitsu, and Yoga. Truth to be told, he's not that great at any of them yet, but he's having a bliss!

It's easier with a community

Wellness journey can be challenging and overwhelming without a community support. There are so much conflicted information out there which can be hard to navigate around. While you are trying out new things to build better habits, if you don't have people around you who don't understand what you are trying to achive it is easy to lose traction and fall back to old habits.

Come join us! Whether you are fighting specific symptoms or simply becoming more conscious of your health, you will find like minded people who are challenging their own limitations in various ways.

Our Services

Wim Hof Method Fundamentals Workshop

In this immersive one day workshop, you will learn the fundamental techniques and information about the Wim Hof Method that you can integrate into your daily life immediately. The workshop will always include breathing and ice bath sessions, and optionally a sauna session as well.

One-on-One Coaching

If you can't make it to the group workshops for any reason, one-on-one sessions are available. The sessions can be customized to fit your exact needs. Whether you are just starting a wellness journey, fighting a disease or looking for a good way to improve your performance, you will get what you are looking for.

Corporation Wellness

Stress management and selfcare are very important for everyone in workforce, and Wim Hof Method is a very effective tool for that. We can provide any combination of presentation, group breathing sessions, and ice bath. You will see your employees get healthier and become more productive. It is a great team building exercise too!
